💡 Study Tips for Maximum Retention

  • Active recall: Cover the definition and try to recall it from the word alone.
  • Spaced repetition: Review Day 1 again on Day 3, and Day 2 on Day 4, etc.
  • Context matters: Notice when each word appears in real-life situations.
  • Use it immediately: Try using 3–5 new words in writing or speech every day.
  • Mark unfamiliar words: Highlight words you find difficult and revisit them daily.
