Day 15 Society, Culture & Human Nouns Words 701–746 · 46 words
society /səˈsaɪəti/ n.
People living together in an organized community.

Daily: We all have a role to play in making society fairer.

Meeting: The impact of this technology on society must be carefully considered.

Daily: Experiencing local culture is the highlight of every trip I take.

Meeting: Building a positive team culture is essential for long-term success.

Meeting: Ongoing education and training are key to retaining top talent.

Meeting: Our decisions should be grounded in science, not assumptions.
