⚡ Quick Start Guide　快速上手指南

Choose your starting point based on your current situation.

🆕 I'm just getting started

  1. Read the 60-Day Plan overview
  2. Record yourself for Day 1 self-assessment
  3. Open NGSL Day 1 and read aloud
  4. Skim 500 Sentences Section 1 & 2
  5. Use one new phrase in conversation today

📅 I have a client meeting soon

  1. Open Client Phrase Bank → your scenario
  2. Read opening phrases aloud 3× each
  3. Review Shadowing Script 1 (status)
  4. Write your 3 key points in English
  5. Say your opening line 5× before the call

👥 I need to improve team English

  1. Open Team Phrase Bank
  2. Study Section 4 (Standup) today
  3. Shadow Script A (Standup) twice
  4. Give tomorrow's standup update in English
  5. Review Code Review phrases this week
💡 Daily Practice Tips　每日练习建议

Consistent daily habits — even just 45 minutes — beat occasional marathon sessions every time.

1

Morning (10 min): Read 5 NGSL words aloud. Make one sentence about today's work for each.

2

Before meetings (5 min): Say your opening phrase 3×. Review 3 key phrases for the topic.

3

Lunch Mon/Thu (15 min): AI role-play using prompts in the shadowing scripts.

4

Evening Tue/Thu (10 min): Record yourself on one topic. Listen. Note one thing to improve.

5

After real meetings (10 min): Write the follow-up email in English using a template.

6

Use the search: On the 500 Sentences page, search in Chinese or English to find phrases instantly.

7

Print what you need: Every page has a Print button. Keep today's materials on your desk.

8

Track milestones: Use the checklist in the 60-Day Plan to see how far you've come.
