Revised Daily Practice Routine (55 min/day)

⚡ Updated (v2.0): Pronunciation now starts on Day 1. Listening added daily. Vocabulary reduced to 10 deep words. Core 30 Phrases added as a dedicated weekly practice.
TimeActivityDurationMaterial
Morning (commute / shower)
From Day 1		 🎧 Listening input — BBC 6 Minute English (Mon/Wed) or BBC The English We Speak (Tue/Thu). Use the 3-step method: listen → transcript → shadow 30 sec. 10 min listening-guide.html
Before work (8:00–8:10)
From Day 1		 🔊 Pronunciation drill — 5 minutes of target sounds from the pronunciation guide. Say each target sound 10×. Then say 3 real sentences using that sound. This is non-negotiable from Day 1 — fixing pronunciation habits early prevents cementing bad ones. 5 min pronunciation-guide.html
Rotate: Week 1–2 = /th/ & /r/, Week 3–4 = word stress, Week 5–6 = connected speech
Before work (8:10–8:20)
10 words/day deep		 📚 NGSL Deep Practice — study 10 NGSL words (not 50). For each: say it aloud, make one sentence about today's actual work, write it down. Next day: recall yesterday's 10 without looking. Quality over quantity. 10 min NGSL day files — 10 words/day = 600 words in 60 days, deeply learned
Before each real meeting
Daily habit		 ⚡ Core 30 warm-up — say 3 phrases from the Core 30 list out loud before any English meeting. Say your planned opening sentence aloud twice. Write your first key point in one English sentence. 5 min core-30-phrases.html + phrase banks
Lunch (Mon/Thu) 🤖 AI role-play — simulate a meeting or team conversation using ChatGPT/Claude. Speak aloud, then type. Ask for corrections. Use this week's Weekly Challenge scenario. 15 min weekly-challenges.html scenarios
Evening (Tue/Thu/Sat) 🎙 Self-recording — use this week's Weekly Challenge prompt. Record 2–3 min, listen back immediately. Note one specific improvement. Record again with that improvement. 10 min Voice Memos app · Keep all recordings for weekly comparison
After real meetings ✉️ Written practice — write the follow-up email or Slack summary in English using a template. Then read one story/article and do the 30-second retell challenge. 10 min email-templates.html · stories.html
